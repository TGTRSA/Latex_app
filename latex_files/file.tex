The integral form of mass conservation is
\begin{equation}
    \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \iiint_{V} \rho \, dV
    + \iint_{S} \rho \mathbf{u} \cdot \mathbf{n} \, dS = 0 .
\end{equation}

For an incompressible fluid with constant density $\rho$, this reduces to
\begin{equation}
    \iint_{S} \mathbf{u} \cdot \mathbf{n} \, dS = 0 .
\end{equation}
